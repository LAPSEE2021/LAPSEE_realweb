Our Vision

Lapsee is dedicated to promoting media literacy in today’s digital age. We want equip our youth with skills and knowldedege necessary to strive in our danger-ridden media landscape.

What We Do

Educational Resources

We create free educational resources related to Media Literacy for parents and teachers to use in educational settings.

Publish News for Kids

We write news stories for kids and allow them gain exposure to international affairs. News stories are written for kids in mind, and the articles are accompanied by reading worksheets to best maximize the learning outcome.

Offer Unique Pedagogy

We write news stories for kids and allow them “to” gain exposure “on” international affairs. News stories are written for kids in mind, and the articles are accompanied by reading worksheets to best maximize the learning outcome.

Host workshops

We host workshops to provide rigorous learning opportunities for students, with topics ranging from digital safety to disinformation.
